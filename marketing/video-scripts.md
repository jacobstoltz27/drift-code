# Drift Video Scripts
## 8 formats × multiple hooks — ready to send to creators

Each script is a minimal framework, not a word-for-word read. The creator fills in
their destination, their personality, their footage. Your brief should say:
**"Here's the structure — make it yours."**

---

## FORMAT 1 — THE AI REVEAL
**Target niche:** Itinerary creators, solo female travelers, digital nomads
**Hook strength:** Very high — product demo in the first 3 seconds
**Optimal length:** 30–45s TikTok / Reels

### Script 1A — "One Sentence" (Fast cut, demo-forward)

```
[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]
On-screen text: "I described my [Japan / Bali / Portugal] trip in one sentence.
This is what it built me."

[SCREEN RECORD or talking head — 0:03–0:20]
Open the Drift AI Planner.
Type your actual dream trip prompt on camera.
Watch the itinerary generate.
Scroll through 2–3 days slowly so the viewer can read it.

[REACTION — 0:20–0:30]
Talk to the camera or do a face reaction:
"Day 4 — I didn't even know this temple existed. It found it for me."

[CTA — 0:30–0:45]
"This is Drift. I've been using it to plan every trip.
Link in bio — it's free to start."
```

**Why it works:** The viewer sees the full product payoff in the first 8 seconds.
No guessing what the app does. The reaction sells the emotion.

---

### Script 1B — "Lazy Traveler" (Relatable, humor-forward)

```
[HOOK — 0:00–0:04]
Talking head:
"I spent 6 hours on Reddit trying to plan my Tokyo trip.
Then I found this."

[DEMO — 0:04–0:18]
Show Drift. Type one sentence prompt.
Fast-forward or speed-ramp the itinerary generating.
"Done. 30 seconds. Seven days."

[COMPARISON — 0:18–0:30]
Split-screen or cut:
Left: Google tabs, Reddit, Notes app chaos.
Right: Drift itinerary, clean and complete.

[CTA — 0:30–0:40]
"It's called Drift. Free to start. I genuinely have no reason to Google
travel stuff anymore."
```

**Why it works:** The "I used to do this hard thing" format is deeply relatable.
The comparison visual is the highest-save format on TikTok.

## FORMAT 2 — THE HIDDEN GEM
**Target niche:** Hidden gems creators, city guide accounts, adventure travel
**Hook strength:** Extremely high — exploits FOMO and discovery emotion
**Optimal length:** 30–60s, can go longer for story-format

### Script 2A — "Nobody Talks About This" (Pure discovery)

```
[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]
B-roll of destination. Text overlay:
"Nobody talks about [Sidemen / Kotor / Tbilisi] and I'm obsessed with it."

[BUILD — 0:03–0:20]
Show 3–4 clips of the destination.
Voiceover: "I found this because Drift has a Hidden Gems section —
it's like a feed of trips that didn't go viral. And that's the whole point."

[Product reveal — 0:20–0:30]
Show the Drift Explore screen, scroll to "Hidden Gems" tab.
Show the specific card that led to the destination.

[Payoff — 0:30–0:45]
"I stole this exact itinerary from someone who'd already been there.
Then I remixed it for my dates and budget."
Show the Steal + Remix flow.

[CTA]
"That's Drift. Link in bio."
```

## FORMAT 3 — THE STEAL
**Target niche:** Anyone with strong travel content — couples, solos, itinerary creators
**Hook strength:** High — "steal" is inherently viral language
**Optimal length:** 30–50s

### Script 3A — "Steal My Entire [Destination] Trip"

```
[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]
Text on screen: "Steal my entire [Portugal / Bali / Japan] itinerary 👇"

[Itinerary walkthrough — 0:03–0:25]
Talk through YOUR trip like you normally would — day by day,
best spots, food, hidden things.
"Day 1 was this. Day 3 we found this by accident..."

[Reveal — 0:25–0:35]
"I keep all my trips on Drift. If you want the full itinerary — every spot,
every note, the exact budget — just look me up on the app and steal it."

[Product demo — 0:35–0:45]
Show your Drift profile. Show the "steal" button on your trip.

[CTA — 0:45–0:50]
"Free on iOS and Android. Link in bio."
```

**Why this works:** The creator is not "doing an ad." They're sharing their trip
like they always do — Drift is just where it lives. Completely organic feel.

## FORMAT 4 — THE TIME CONTRAST (Before / After)
**Target niche:** Productivity-meets-travel creators, digital nomads, "realistic travel" accounts
**Hook strength:** Very high — universally relatable pain point
**Optimal length:** 20–35s (fast-paced)

### Script 4A — "30 Seconds vs. 3 Weeks"

```
[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]
Text overlay on chaotic planning footage (tabs, notes, Reddit):
"Planning my last trip: 3 weeks. Planning this one: 30 seconds."

[Before — 0:03–0:10]
Quick cuts of old planning method: Google Docs, TripAdvisor tabs,
texting friends in a group chat, Pinterest boards.

[After — 0:10–0:20]
Open Drift. Type prompt. Itinerary done.
Speed ramp with satisfying music hit.

[Beat — 0:20–0:28]
"This is Drift. One sentence. Full trip."
Show 2–3 itinerary days scrolling.

[CTA — 0:28–0:35]
"Free. Link in bio."
```

## FORMAT 5 — THE TRAVEL PROFILE / SCORE
**Target niche:** "Country counter" creators, passport collectors, competitive travelers
**Hook strength:** High — gamification triggers strong curiosity
**Optimal length:** 20–35s

### Script 5A — "Drift Told Me I've Explored X% of the World"

```
[HOOK — 0:00–0:04]
Phone screen: Drift profile showing world map and travel score.
Text/voiceover: "This app just told me I've explored [X]% of the world.
Now I want to know yours."

[Profile walkthrough — 0:04–0:20]
Show the Drift profile — world map highlighting visited countries,
travel score, trip count, countries, cities.
"My score is [X]. Apparently that's [Adventurous / Building / Excellent]."

[Challenge hook — 0:20–0:30]
"Drop your score in the comments. I want to see who's beating me."

[CTA]
"Free on iOS and Android — link in bio."
```

**Why this works:** Competition + community comment section = watch time + saves + shares.
This format is the most viral of all five because it generates conversation.
