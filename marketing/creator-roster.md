## THE 20-SECOND TEST — RUN THIS BEFORE EVERY OUTREACH

**Check 1: Views, not followers.** Open their last 10 videos. What's the average view count?
A creator with 80K followers averaging 400K views beats a 500K creator averaging 20K views.
You're paying for the video, not the bio.

**Check 2: Comments.** Open a recent video. Are people asking "where is this?" / "can you share
your itinerary?" / "saving this for my trip"? That's your buyer. Emoji-only comments = dead audience.

**Check 3: Parasocial relationship.** Watch 60 seconds of any video. Does the camera feel like
a window into their actual travel life? Would their audience take a recommendation from them?

If any of the three is a no — pass and move on.

## CREATORS TO AVOID (Apply the Cal AI Lesson)

These might seem tempting but the audiences won't convert.

| Creator Type | Why They Won't Convert | Cal AI Parallel |
|---|---|---|
| Mega luxury accounts (5M+ followers) | Audience watches to fantasize, not plan. They aren't booking these trips. | Big brand overpay trap |
| Travel deal / miles / points creators | Hyper price-sensitive audience, rewards-focused. Drift is a $8.99/mo planning tool. | Wrong product-audience fit |
| Celebrity travel content | Audience is there for the celebrity, not the destination. | UFC fighters |
| Pure cinematography accounts (no talking head) | Beautiful content, zero parasocial relationship. No trust transfer. | Comedy viral creators |
| Family travel with young children | Wrong life stage. Planning Disney trips, not solo Bali trips. | Wrong audience |
| Travel booking / deals aggregators | Audience expects discounts and deals, not a planning app | Wrong intent |

## HOW TO FIND MORE CREATORS (DIY Discovery)

**TikTok:**
1. Search: "solo travel [destination]", "hidden gems [city]", "travel itinerary [country]"
2. Sort: Most played
3. Filter: Creator tab
4. Apply the 20-second test on the top 20 results
5. Repeat for 5 different search queries per niche

**Instagram:**
1. Search: #solotravel, #hiddengems, #travelitinerary, #digitalnomad
2. Filter: Top Posts
3. Click through to profiles
4. Apply the 20-second test

**Creator discovery tools (free tier):**
- [Creator.co](https://creator.co) — Influencer marketplace, search by niche + size
- [Collabstr](https://collabstr.com) — Direct booking, rates listed upfront
- [AspireIQ](https://aspireiq.com) — More enterprise, but free trial available
- [Social Cat](https://thesocialcat.com) — Travel niche specifically, creator database

**What to type in DMs:**
"Hey [Name] — watched your [specific video], the [specific detail] was amazing.
I'm on the team at Drift — travel app where you discover trips from creators and use AI to plan your own.
Think it'd be natural for your feed. Cool if I send the details?"

Four lines. One CTA. That's it.
