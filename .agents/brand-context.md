# Brand Context — Drift

> Foundation file. Every brand/marketing/creative skill reads this FIRST and treats it as the source of truth. When this file and a skill's built-in "defaults" disagree, THIS FILE WINS.

## 1. Brand Basics

- **App / brand name:** **Drift** (this never changes).
- **AI concierge name:** **Peregrine** — the app's AI travel concierge. Drift is the product; Peregrine is the intelligent guide *inside* it. Refer to the concierge as "Peregrine," not "the AI" or "Drift's assistant," in user-facing copy.
- **One-line:** Drift is an AI-powered travel app where **Peregrine**, your personal travel concierge, designs taste-driven itineraries and surfaces experiences you'd never have found alone.
- **Category:** consumer travel — inspiration + planning (NOT booking).
- **Stage:** early / pre-scale.
- **Paid product:** **Drift Plus** — the only paid upgrade. Do not invent other tiers or pricing.

## 2. What Drift Is (and Isn't)

- **Is:** an AI travel *concierge* experience — personalized itineraries, hidden-gem discovery, a social **trip feed**, a proprietary **Trip Score** (0–100), invite/viral loops, and premium taste.
- **Is NOT — hard guardrails (never violate in any copy, ad, or creative):**
  - **Drift is NOT a booking platform.** Never say or imply *book, reserve, reservations, hotels/flights as inventory, checkout, payments, deals, discounts on travel*. Drift inspires and plans; it does not sell travel inventory.
  - Drift Plus is the only paid product — no invented pricing or tiers.

## 3. Peregrine — the AI Concierge

Peregrine is the branded persona of Drift's AI. It has its own identity that should come through in product copy, ad UGC "voice of the concierge," and visuals.

- **Meaning:** *peregrine* = "wanderer / traveler / pilgrim." A peregrine falcon is the fastest animal alive (a stoop dive near ~240 mph), a precision hunter with extraordinary eyesight that migrates vast distances.
- **Persona traits:** precise, instinctive, effortlessly fast, well-traveled, sees what others miss, calm mastery. Never frantic, never a chatty "assistant."
- **Personality words:** insider · precise · effortless · worldly · a little wry.
- **Positioning of the concierge:** "Peregrine plans with a falcon's instinct — spotting the one perfect place in a city the moment you land."

### Peregrine visual mark (logo the founder provided)
- A **minimalist, monochrome (black-on-white) line-art peregrine falcon in flight**, wings raised on the upstroke, gliding on a dynamic diagonal, sharp hooked beak, focused eye, clean feather detailing. Emblem / tattoo-line style; a single confident outline weight. Reads as an app icon and a monochrome mark.
- **Brand motifs to reuse:** flight, the upswept wing silhouette, the stoop/dive line, speed streaks, a sharp focused eye, precision. Use the falcon as a recurring graphic device and app-icon shape.

## 4. Audience

- **Primary:** aspirational millennial / Gen-Z travelers — "spontaneous but discerning." They want extraordinary, non-touristy trips without the planning grind.
- **Key secondary segments:** group-trip organizers (the friend who plans for everyone), couples planning the once-a-year big trip, solo explorers, luxury-on-a-budget travelers.
- **The problem Drift solves:** planning a genuinely great trip is fragmented, generic, and exhausting (20 tabs, group-chat chaos, blogs that all recommend the same 10 tourist spots).
- **Audience language:** "hidden gems," "like a local," "off the beaten path," "I don't want the tourist stuff," "someone just plan it for me," "trip of a lifetime."

## 5. Positioning

- **Statement:** *For discerning travelers who want an extraordinary trip without the planning grind, Drift is the AI travel concierge — powered by Peregrine — that designs a taste-matched itinerary of places you'd never find alone. Unlike generic AI, travel blogs, or booking apps, Drift plans with a local's instinct and a falcon's precision — and never tries to sell you a reservation.*
- **Main alternatives (and how we differ):** generic AI chat (Drift is purpose-built, tasteful, and structured), travel blogs/listicles (Drift is personalized and non-touristy), booking apps (Drift plans the *experience*, doesn't sell inventory), spreadsheets/manual planning (Drift is effortless).
- **Market tier:** premium / luxury feel, broadly accessible.

## 6. Voice & Tone (Peregrine's voice)

- **Voice:** Condé Nast Traveler concierge meets a well-connected local fixer. Aspirational, effortless, insider, a touch wry.
- **Do:** evoke feeling and arrival; be specific and worldly; sound like someone who's *been there*.
- **Don't:** be salesy, use hype/emoji-spam, write listicle clickbait, or use booking/transaction language.

## 7. Visual Identity

- **Theme:** luxury, dark, cinematic. Archetype: luxury.
- **Colors:** background near-black `#070B14` / deep navy `#1B1F3B`; primary accent electric indigo `#5B67FF`; secondary accent teal `#14B8A6`; text `#F5F7FA` / muted `#A1A1AA`; glass surface `rgba(27,31,59,0.6)`.
- **Effects:** glassmorphism, soft indigo/teal glow, generous negative space, 8pt grid, rounded-2xl/24px radii.
- **Typography:** **Satoshi** headlines (black weight, tight tracking) / **Inter** body; uppercase micro-labels with wide `0.2em` tracking.
- **Imagery:** cinematic editorial travel photography, dark moody grade, single accent light in indigo/teal, glass-UI overlays. Avoid stocky, oversaturated, cluttered visuals. The Peregrine falcon mark is the signature graphic device.

## 8. Product Surfaces (for message-match)

Tabs/flows in the app: onboarding (preferences) · home · planner (Peregrine generates itineraries) · trips · explore · social feed · profile · invite. Signature features: **Peregrine**-generated itineraries, **Trip Score**, "Steal/Remix Itinerary" (a Drift Plus feature), invite/viral loop.
