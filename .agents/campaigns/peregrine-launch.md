# Campaign — Launch Peregrine (Drift's AI travel companion)

*Orchestrated by `campaign-builder`. Stages: [strategy](../strategy/peregrine-launch.md) → [creative](../creative/peregrine-launch.md) → [ads](../ads/peregrine-launch.md) → landing → email → launch (below).*

## Campaign one-pager
- **Goal:** app installs + first **"Ask Peregrine"** activation.
- **Audience:** "The Overwhelmed Dreamer" (24–38, aspirational, dreads planning); + group organizers, couples, solo explorers.
- **The hook:** *Land like you already know the place.*
- **Lead angle:** **"Ask Peregrine."** (Siri-style verb) + the falcon.
- **Success metric:** % of new installs who complete a **first Peregrine itinerary within 24h**. Supporting: CPI, hook rate, D1 retention.
- **Concept (locked):** *"Ask the Sky"* — Peregrine as your falcon guide.

---
## Landing / destination
**Above the fold (the two-brand pitch):**
- Kicker: `MEET PEREGRINE`
- H1: **Ask Peregrine. Land like you already know the place.**
- Sub: *Peregrine is your AI travel companion inside Drift — the social travel platform. Ask it anything: plan a trip, find hidden gems, fit a budget. It plans; you go.*
- Primary CTA: **Get Drift — free** · secondary: "See Peregrine in action" (scrolls to demo).
- Hero visual: Blue Hour Arrival + falcon (creative hero #1).

**Section order:**
1. **The ask, animated** — a chat: "Plan 5 days in Tokyo, food + design, $2k" → itinerary blooms (message-matches REEL #1).
2. **Three pillars** — *Ask, don't research · Local instinct, not tourist lists · Shaped to you.* (icon + line each)
3. **Not the tourist stuff** — hidden-gem proof strip (message-matches CAROUSEL #1).
4. **It lives in Drift** — the platform: share itineraries, follow travelers, plan with friends, Trip Score.
5. **Objection-handler** — "Isn't this just ChatGPT?" → purpose-built, current, structured, shareable.
6. **Drift Plus** — the only paid upgrade (Remix/Steal Itinerary, power features). No pressure; core Peregrine is free.
7. **Final CTA** — *Ask Peregrine. Get Drift, free.*

**FAQ:** Is it free? (Yes; Plus is optional.) · Does it book anything? (No — Drift plans experiences, never sells bookings.) · How is it different from ChatGPT? · Does it know non-touristy places? · What cities? · iOS/Android?

> **Before scaling spend, run `uiux-critic` on the real onboarding + first-Ask flow** — every ad promises "ask and it plans," so the install→first-itinerary path must deliver that in under a minute or CPI-to-activation leaks.

## Email / lifecycle (post-install + waitlist)
1. **Welcome — "Meet Peregrine."** The one thing to do: ask your first question. One CTA → open chat. (Drives activation.)
2. **Value/proof — "Places you'd never find alone."** 3 real hidden-gem examples; nudge a second ask.
3. **Objection-buster — "Not another travel blog."** Why Peregrine ≠ generic AI; show a shaped-to-you itinerary.
4. **Depth/urgency — "Plan the big one."** Prompt to plan their actual next trip; introduce Drift Plus (Remix). Soft, optional.
- On-voice (concierge, wry, insider), same hook throughout.

## Launch plan
**Timeline**
- **Pre-launch (T-2 wks):** teaser — the falcon mark + "Ask the sky. Coming to Drift." Build a waitlist; seed 10–20 travel creators for UGC (skeptic + organizer archetypes).
- **Launch week:** turn on Meta (3 TOF hero angles), publish creator UGC, email the waitlist, in-app announcement of Peregrine.
- **Post (wks 2–4):** scale winners, spin variations of the winning angle, retarget MOF/BOF, layer in "in-the-moment" use (REEL #3).

**Channel schedule:** Meta (paid, lead) · organic IG/TikTok (creator UGC + reveal reels) · email (waitlist → activation) · in-app (existing users meet Peregrine).

**Metric per stage:** pre = waitlist signups + creator briefs out · launch = CPI + hook rate + install→first-itinerary · post = activation rate + D1/D7 retention + CAC on scaled winners.

**Go/no-go checklist:** ☐ install→first-Ask flow passes `uiux-critic` ☐ 3 TOF creatives + variations live ☐ tracking on install + "first itinerary" event ☐ landing message-matches ads ☐ Peregrine handles the ad-promised queries well ☐ spelling of Peregrine/Peregrin locked.

## Sequenced action plan
1. **Ship first:** STATIC #1 (pain), UGC #1 (skeptic), REEL #1 (reveal) — the three most distinct TOF angles.
2. **Judge by:** hook rate/thumbstop → CTR → CPI, then install→first-itinerary activation.
3. **Scale rule:** a winning angle → spin 5 variations (same angle, new hook/visual); losers → swap the hook, keep the visual; kill only after a fair test.
4. **QA gate:** don't scale spend until the destination flow passes `uiux-critic`.
