# Creative Direction — Launch Peregrine

*Produced by `creative-director` from the strategy. Locks one concept so ads + landing share a look.*

## Big idea (LOCKED): **"Ask the Sky"**
Peregrine is your falcon guide. Every asset frames the AI as an instinctive presence that already knows the way — you ask, and the line-mark falcon traces a path across the sky/map and lands on the one perfect place. The hook it serves: *land like you already know the place.*

*Alternates held for testing:* "The Line" (pure route-tracing minimalism) · "In Hand" (app-moment led).

## Visual direction
Cinematic, dark, luxury restraint. Real travelers in real places at **blue hour / magic hour**. The **peregrine falcon line-mark** appears as a subtle overlay tracing a route or gliding above the scene, then resolving to a map pin. Indigo/teal glow as the only saturated color against near-black. Never busy, never gamified, never "deal" energy.

## Photography / imagery style
Editorial travel photography, 35mm feel, shallow depth of field, moody film grade. Human-in-place (a figure small against a grand setting) more than faces. Warm practical lights (lanterns, windows, streetlife) against cool near-black skies. Avoid stocky, oversaturated, cluttered, top-10-list visuals.

## Motion references (for reels/stories)
Smooth gliding camera (falcon-POV drift), calm pace with **one accelerando** on the reveal: a typed question → the itinerary "blooms" on screen. Screen-record moments of the Peregrine chat should feel effortless and fast. Captions always on; sound-on design.

## Layout
- Ratios: **4:5** (feed), **9:16** (story/reel), **1:1** (carousel).
- Text-safe zone: **top third** (or lower third for scene-heavy shots). Headline in Satoshi black, tight tracking; a wide-tracked uppercase label for the kicker.
- One idea per frame. The falcon line-mark is the recurring signature.

## Color palette
- Background near-black `#070B14` / deep navy `#1B1F3B`
- Accent electric indigo `#5B67FF` (primary signal, CTAs, glow)
- Secondary teal `#14B8A6` (support glow, route line)
- Text `#F5F7FA` / muted `#A1A1AA`

## Typography
**Satoshi** headlines (black weight, tight tracking) · **Inter** body · uppercase micro-labels at `0.2em` tracking (e.g. `MEET PEREGRINE`).

## Iconography / graphic system
The **line-art peregrine falcon** (wings raised, in flight) is the hero device. Supporting motifs: a thin **route line** that arcs and lands on a **map pin**; faint **speed streaks** on the dive. Use the falcon as app-icon shape and as the "cursor" that guides the eye through each ad.

## Moodboards (3 directions)
1. **Blue Hour Arrival** — lone traveler, grand setting, magic-hour sky, falcon gliding above; warmth below, cool above. *Thesis: the feeling of arriving somewhere that already feels yours.*
2. **The Line** — near-black canvas, the falcon's route line arcing over a stylized map/coast, landing on a glowing pin. *Thesis: instinct finds the one perfect place.*
3. **In Hand** — intimate night shots of the phone showing the Peregrine chat/itinerary, bokeh streetlife behind. *Thesis: the magic is one question away.*
