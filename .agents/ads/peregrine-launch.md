# Meta Ad Batch — Launch Peregrine

*Produced by `meta-ad-creative` from strategy + creative. A launch **starter matrix** — 3 fully-specified ads per format across angles + funnel stages. Each format expands to the full 20 on request. CTA defaults to **Download** (BOF) / **Learn More** (TOF-MOF). No booking/transaction language anywhere.*

## STATIC (feed 4:5)

### STATIC #1 — Pain · TOF
- **Hook:** 20 tabs open. Still no plan.
- **Primary text:** Planning the trip is the worst part of the trip. Ask Peregrine instead — Drift's AI travel companion turns "I want to go to Lisbon" into a real day-by-day itinerary in minutes.
- **Headline:** Ask Peregrine. Skip the 20 tabs.
- **CTA:** Learn More
- **Visual:** Split feel — chaotic browser-tab clutter dissolving into one clean dark itinerary card; faint falcon line-mark gliding across the seam.
- **Audience:** interest: travel planning, Wanderlog/TripAdvisor, solo travel; broad TOF.
- **Testing hypothesis:** If we lead with the *pain of planning* (not the app), cold thumbstop rises because the problem is instantly recognizable.
- **🖼 AI prompt:** "Dark near-black composition, left side a messy cluster of faded browser tabs, right side a single glowing indigo itinerary card; a thin line-art peregrine falcon glides across the middle. 4:5, text-safe top third."

### STATIC #2 — Feature-as-magic · MOF
- **Hook:** "Find hidden beaches in Greece." Done.
- **Primary text:** Ask Peregrine like you'd ask a local who actually knows. Hidden gems, not tourist lists — shaped to your taste and budget.
- **Headline:** Local instinct, on demand.
- **CTA:** Download
- **Visual:** Phone chat bubble with that exact question, answer blooming into a mini itinerary; secluded Greek cove at blue hour behind.
- **Audience:** MOF retargeting: engaged with previous ads / visited landing.
- **Testing hypothesis:** If we show a concrete, enviable query + instant result, MOF CTR rises via proof of the "instinct" claim.
- **🖼 AI prompt:** Hero "In Hand" prompt, swapped to a hidden Greek cove reflected in the scene.

### STATIC #3 — Identity · TOF
- **Hook:** For people who travel like they mean it.
- **Primary text:** Not the tourist stuff. Peregrine plans trips with a falcon's eye for the places worth flying for.
- **Headline:** Land like a local.
- **CTA:** Learn More
- **Visual:** Brand-mark hero — line-art falcon, indigo/teal glow, near-black; wordmark PEREGRINE / inside Drift.
- **Audience:** lookalike of engaged travelers; aspirational broad.
- **Testing hypothesis:** If we sell identity + brand, we build a memorable falcon association even without a click.
- **🖼 AI prompt:** Hero "Brand Mark" prompt (9:16 → cropped 4:5).

## UGC (reel/story scripts, phone-shot, authentic)

### UGC #1 — Skeptic → convert · TOF
- **Hook (spoken):** "I hate planning trips. Like, genuinely."
- **Script:** problem in her words (group chat chaos) → *"then my friend told me to just ask Peregrine"* → screen-record: types "plan 5 days in Tokyo, love food + design, $2k" → itinerary blooms → "…it knew the tiny coffee places. Not the tourist ones." → soft CTA "It's the Drift app."
- **Headline:** I asked. It planned. That's it.
- **CTA:** Download
- **Audience:** broad TOF, women 24–34, travel + lifestyle.
- **Testing hypothesis:** If a skeptic-to-believer arc leads, authenticity beats polish and TOF hold rate rises.
- **🖼 AI/production note:** authentic selfie-style, imperfect handheld, real phone screen-record. NOT a commercial.

### UGC #2 — Group organizer · MOF
- **Hook:** "POV: you're the friend who always plans the trip."
- **Script:** the burden ("everyone has opinions, no one plans") → asks Peregrine to fit everyone's vibe + budget → shares the itinerary straight to the group → relief. CTA: "Let Peregrine be the planner friend."
- **CTA:** Download
- **Audience:** MOF + interest: group travel, bachelorette/friends trips.
- **Testing hypothesis:** If we cast Peregrine as "the planner friend," the organizer segment converts on relief.

### UGC #3 — Budget · TOF
- **Hook:** "Make this trip fit $2,000. Watch."
- **Script:** screen-record reshaping an itinerary to budget in real time; genuine surprise it kept the good stuff. CTA soft.
- **CTA:** Learn More
- **Testing hypothesis:** If budget-control is the lead, price-conscious travelers engage (defuses "is it worth it").
